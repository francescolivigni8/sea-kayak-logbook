# App Store Metadata Draft

## App Name

Your Kayaking Journal

## Subtitle

Log, plan, and review your paddling

## Promotional Text

A private paddling journal for logging sessions, planning routes, reviewing conditions, and building course-ready reports from real time on the water.

## Keywords

kayak, sea kayak, paddling, paddle log, route planner, expedition, training log, marine weather

## Description

Your Kayaking Journal helps paddlers keep one reliable record of time on the water.

Use it to:

- log quick or detailed paddling sessions
- save launch areas, places, and route traces
- plan future outings with route and weather context
- organize sessions into folders
- attach GPX, FIT, and photo files
- review distance, conditions, development, and consistency over time
- generate structured reports for coaching, courses, or assessments

The app is designed for real-world paddling use: simple enough for a quick session entry, but detailed enough for deeper reflection and structured reporting when needed.
